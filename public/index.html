About Chandan Sarees

With over 25 years of experience in the textile industry, Chandan Sarees has been a trusted name for authentic Indian attire. We specialize in handpicked sarees, lehengas, and traditional wear that celebrates the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship.

Our collection features:

  • Traditional handwork and embroidery
  • Modern designs with classic appeal
  • Competitive pricing
  • Excellent customer service
